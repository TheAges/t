Brief
I collaborated with Sunprime and Runelab to design a tailor-made platform to streamline their workflow, document handling, and project tracking. Given the complex approval processes and long administrative timelines they face, the system needed to support multi-project oversight, compliance tracking, and external communication. The Challenge

Sunprime, a company specializing in the development and implementation of renewable energy projects, faced growing challenges in managing the complexity of its operations. With multiple projects running in parallel, each requiring close coordination across administrative, legal, and technical departments, their existing workflow had become increasingly inefficient.

The company relied heavily on manual processes, spreadsheets, and a patchwork of non-integrated tools to track project progress, manage documentation, and communicate across teams. As the scale and volume of their operations grew, these tools began to show critical limitations. It became difficult to maintain visibility into project statuses and ensure reliable performance.

What further complicated matters was the long and unpredictable nature of administrative and legal approval timelines. Each project involved the generation, review, and submission of numerous documents, often with input from several external stakeholders, including public authorities, partners, and legal entities. Without a unified system, important data and files were scattered, and tracking the status of these interactions became a time-consuming burden.

Sunprime approached us to help design a system that would support their operational expansion by addressing these core problems. Their goal was to centralize project oversight, simplify compliance tracking, and streamline internal and external communication.

Research & Process Mapping

To ensure the platform addressed real operational challenges and solved the company's real-world issues, I led a comprehensive research phase focused on understanding Sunprime's workflows in detail. Given the complexity of their projects and the strict regulatory environment they operate in, it was critical to map out not only the internal operations but also the external interactions that influence project success.

I began with stakeholder interviews, engaging directly with key figures across Sunprime's organization, including project managers, field technicians, and administrative staff. These conversations provided first-hand insights into the tools they currently used, the pain points they encountered on a daily basis, and the specific needs of each department throughout a project's lifecycle.

From there, I conducted process analysis sessions with them to break down how projects moved through different stages: planning, development, approval, and execution. This revealed several friction points:

  • Difficulty maintaining an overview of project statuses and deadlines.
  • Administrative delays caused by fragmented documentation and approval chains.
  • Lack of centralized communication between internal teams and external stakeholders (e.g., government offices, service providers).

To further support the design, I completed resource and workflow mapping to identify every document, approval, and compliance check required throughout each phase. This step was crucial in designing a platform that could not only track project progression but also anticipate the documentation and actions needed at each milestone.

This research phase served as the foundation for defining the platform's architecture, ensuring that every feature aligned with real use cases and would ultimately make their operations more manageable, transparent, and efficient.

Platform Structure and Information Architecture

Grounded in the insights gathered from stakeholder interviews and process mapping, we translated Sunprime's operational complexity into a structured, intuitive platform. The design focused on reducing friction in project tracking while providing visibility into critical workflows and documentation. We defined the core interaction model and data architecture, organizing the system around key functional areas that mirrored Sunprime's internal processes.

The Dashboard served as the entry point, offering a high-level overview of all ongoing projects, current statuses, and critical notifications. To complement this, a Map View allowed users to locate and monitor projects geographically, an essential feature that reflected how Sunprime's project managers organized their work, given the distributed nature of their operations across different regions.

Dashboard and Map View: a high-level overview of every project, with a geographic view to locate and monitor them across regions.

Each project was accessible through a detailed Project View, segmented into the real-world pipeline stages the client followed. These sections included:

  • Project Description (property registry data, project manager info, etc.)
  • Lead Management
  • Project Development
  • Operations
  • Construction
  • Project Costs
  • Budget Overview

This breakdown allowed teams to navigate and manage project data logically, while ensuring that all required documentation and actions were traceable within their respective phases.

above: Project View: the detailed sidebar, segmented into the real-world pipeline stages the client followed. below: A project's data organised by phase, with documentation and actions traceable within each one.

A dedicated Project Status Module featured a custom "traffic light" visualization, offering an at-a-glance understanding of a project's health and urgency, this was an explicit request of the PMs teams, that used a similar visualization to manage and communicate internally the state of the various phases of each project.

Project Status Module: a custom traffic-light visualisation of each project's health and urgency, mirroring the system the PMs already used internally

Another crucial part of the platform was the Advanced Search & Filtering System, supporting complex queries using Boolean logic. This empowered users, especially the management team to quickly retrieve projects or documents based on multiple criteria, saving time and reducing manual effort.

Advanced Search and Filtering: complex queries with Boolean logic, letting the management team retrieve projects and documents on multiple criteria at once

Finally, based on initial research findings, we introduced a Sidebar Tool for writing project-specific notes, setting reminders, and linking them directly with the calendar and deadlines. This feature played a key role in enabling proactive project management and reducing reliance on external tools or personal note-taking systems.

Given the intricacy of the platform, I created detailed high-fidelity wireframes early in the process, which were continuously validated with Sunprime's internal teams. This allowed us to refine workflows, confirm feature accuracy, and ensure the platform directly addressed their daily operational needs before moving into development.

UI & Prototype Development

After defining the structure and user flows, I created detailed wireframes to map out the platform's core screens, including the dashboard, project sections, and scheduling tools. These wireframes ensured alignment with operational needs and were key to validating usability before development.

I also produced a visual sample for the dashboard to explore typography, and color palette and the overall design system that aligned with Sunprime's brand identity while maintaining a clear and intuitive design. The design emphasized clarity, hierarchy, and quick access to key information, setting the foundation for a clean and functional interface.

High fidelity Mockup of the platform, used to show the final look and feel of the solution
Outcome & Impact

The project led to the development of a custom platform designed specifically around Sunprime's complex workflows, long approval processes, and strict regulatory requirements. From the start, I took the lead in translating user needs into a functional and flexible system that could centralize project tracking, manage large volumes of documents, and monitor deadlines with ease.

Working closely with Sunprime's internal teams, project managers, legal staff, and administrators, as well as our developers, I ensured that the platform architecture and features aligned with real operational needs. Every module, from project phases to payment tracking, was shaped to match how the teams actually worked.

Even with a tight timeline and technical complexity, the outcome was a scalable and easy-to-use solution that helped Sunprime reduce administrative friction, stay on top of deadlines, and better manage multiple ongoing projects.
